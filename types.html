Types Of Forex Trading

✅ Scalping
✅ Day Trading
✅ Swing Trading

Scalping

Traders hold trades for a short period of time and take profits. Traders open big lot sizes to catch a few pips and leave the markets. They usually trade these times frames below 15 Minute i.e m5 and m1. They are many scalping strategies traders use and am going to discuss them in here. When scalping, a trader starts analyzing the markets from H4 and scales down to the entry time frame which can be m1 or m5.

Day Trading

Short-term trading strategy that focuses on the buying and selling of currency pairs within the same trading day. Traders tend to hold trades for hours during the day. Trader chooses the best trading session. Chooses a right strategy, these strategies can be based on news, trend and momentum trading as we are going to discuss.

Swing Trading

Swing trading involves taking trades that last a couple of days up to several months in order to profit from an anticipated price move. Traders hold trades for a long period of time. Swing traders look for swing highs and swing lows and execute trades at those points.
